## How It Works

1. **Install** Audit Monkee from the GoHighLevel Marketplace
2. **Select** audit types (SEO, Design, Tech Stack, or All)
3. **Choose** target contact or enter website URL
4. **Run** audit - results appear in seconds
5. **Review** comprehensive findings in contact record
